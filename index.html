Biography

Sungkwang Mun received the B.S. degree in computer engineering from Kwangwoon University, Seoul, Korea in 2007 under the advise of Dr. Seongwon Lee and Ph.D. degree in electrical and computer engineering from Mississippi State university, Starkville, Mississippi in December of 2012 under the advise of Dr. James E. Fowler.

He was a graduate researcher in the Geosystems Research Institute (GRI) at the Mississippi State HPC². Main focus of the research was to seek engineering oriented solution for the reconstruction problem for Compress Sensing, especially of natural image and video. The current focus includes quantization problem of compressed sensing for reconstruction problem of magnetic resonance image.
