Investigating efficient data acquisition and reconstruction using Compressed Sensing (CS) frameworks. This research focuses on multiscale block-based sampling and iterative reconstruction algorithms for high-resolution imaging and dynamic video sequences.

Molecular Dynamic Simulations
Developing high-fidelity interatomic potentials and parallel molecular dynamics frameworks to study the interface and mechanical properties of composite materials. This work enables the predictive modeling of polymers, hydrocarbons, and complex metallic alloys at the atomic scale.

Advanced Manufacturing and Maintenance
Focusing on the development of digital twins and predictive maintenance strategies using physics-informed neural networks (PINNs) for advanced manufacturing systems. This includes real-time monitoring of additive manufacturing processes and the implementation of PHM (Prognostics and Health Management) frameworks.
