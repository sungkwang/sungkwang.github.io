Research Themes

Computational Image & Video Processing
Investigating efficient data acquisition and reconstruction using **Compressed Sensing** frameworks. This research focuses on multiscale block-based sampling and iterative reconstruction algorithms for high-resolution imaging and dynamic video sequences.
[ BCS-SPL Project ] [ Related Publications ]
Multiscale Modeling of Organo-Metallic Systems
Developing high-fidelity interatomic potentials and parallel molecular dynamics frameworks to study the interface and mechanical properties of composite materials. This work enables the predictive modeling of polymers, hydrocarbons, and complex metallic alloys at the atomic scale.
[ MEAM-BO Framework ] [ Related Publications ]
AI-Driven Design Optimization
Utilizing machine learning surrogate models and physics-informed neural networks (PINNs) to optimize additive manufacturing processes and structural designs. Our frameworks aim to reduce the computational cost of design exploration in high-dimensional spaces.
